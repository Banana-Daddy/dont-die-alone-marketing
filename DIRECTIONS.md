# Don't Die Alone — 4 Marketing Page Directions

PRO-MAX MODE locked. Each direction targets a different *type* of guy — not just a different palette.

---

## Direction A — LOCKER ROOM
*Friday-night-football-meets-sports-betting-Sunday energy. The guy who watches RedZone.*

- **Mood:** pre-game scoreboard energy. Loud, scored, confident. Numbers as truth.
- **Fonts:** Barlow Condensed (display) + Barlow (body)
- **Palette:**
  - `#0A0A0A` Off-court black (bg)
  - `#FAFAFA` Floodlight white (text)
  - `#FBBF24` Scoreboard amber (CTA / numbers)
  - `#DC2626` Ref-stripe red (warnings / "fuck-boy detector")
- **Layout:** Scoreboard hero (your match-rate vs. league average), bench-card features, post-game-show stat highlights, ref-stripe section dividers. No card-shadows — flat-panel like a stadium board.
- **Signature move:** A live-flipping "scoreboard" hero. The 0.6% gut-punch stat presented like a Friday-night football board, glowing amber LED on black panel.
- **Killing:** stock-photo "smiling guy with phone." We don't show people. We show numbers.
- **Inspiration refs (21st.dev):** Hero with bold tracking-tight typography (`Hero 04`-style)

## Direction B — EDITORIAL
*A New Yorker piece on why dating is broken. The guy who reads The Verge and Esquire.*

- **Mood:** quarterly arts journal meets longread investigation. Refined, smart, dry-humored.
- **Fonts:** Libre Bodoni (display, italic-leaning) + Public Sans (body)
- **Palette:**
  - `#FAFAFA` Newsprint cream (bg)
  - `#09090B` Ink black (text)
  - `#EC4899` Brand pink (accent — kept from product)
  - `#3F3F46` Graphite (secondary text)
- **Layout:** magazine spread. Drop cap intro, pull quotes set in italic Bodoni at scale, byline-style attribution ("Reporting by the algorithm. Editing by you."), footnote-style citations. Hairline drop-rules between sections.
- **Signature move:** Issue 001 cover treatment. Headline set as a real publication cover line, with a tiny tracked-out kicker (THE DATING ISSUE / VOL. 1 / WINTER 2026).
- **Killing:** SaaS dashboard look. No gradient buttons, no card-shadows, no purple-to-pink. Newsprint discipline.
- **Inspiration refs (21st.dev):** Editorial display heros with massive serif type, hairline-rule dividers, side-byline blocks

## Direction C — ARCADE
*Pizza-shop arcade cabinet rebrand. The chronically-online guy who quotes One Punch Man.*

- **Mood:** Y2K chrome and dive-bar neon. Character-select fighting-game energy.
- **Fonts:** Anton (display) + Epilogue (body)
- **Palette:**
  - `#0A0A0A` CRT black (bg)
  - `#EC4899` Hot pink neon (primary)
  - `#22D3EE` Electric cyan (accent)
  - `#FACC15` Lemon yellow (highlight / score)
- **Layout:** Character-select fighting-game UI. INSERT COIN hero, lifebars instead of stat cards, marquee PRESS START tickers. CRT scanline overlay across the page.
- **Signature move:** Lifebar-style score readout. Your dating-profile score rendered as a fighting-game health bar that fills based on rubric scores. Plus a 32px banana-suit pixel sprite as a hidden Easter-egg "founder cameo" (Daddy in 16-bit form).
- **Killing:** corporate cleanliness. Embrace chunky pixel borders, hard CRT noise, neon glow.
- **Inspiration refs (21st.dev):** Pixel-rocket-voyager-style retro hero, gaming login (NexusGate-style backdrop), pixel-button micro-interactions

## Direction D — QUIET
*Calm.app for dating sanity. The introspective guy who's quietly tired.*

- **Mood:** thoughtful, paced, monochromatic. Almost-zine, almost-essay, definitely-not-marketing.
- **Fonts:** Lora (display, serif) + Raleway (body, geometric sans)
- **Palette:**
  - `#FAFAF7` Warm off-white (bg)
  - `#0A0A0A` Deep ink (text)
  - `#A3B18A` Soft sage (accent)
  - `#C9A66B` Muted clay (secondary accent)
- **Layout:** one column, generous margins, single-column reading flow. No nav clutter. Stats reveal small, almost as footnotes. Single sentence in italic serif as the entire opening hero.
- **Signature move:** **Blank-page first impression.** Just one sentence in serif italic centered on the page when you land. Stats reveal as you scroll, presented like footnotes from a memoir. The opposite of every other direction.
- **Killing:** hype, exclamation marks, urgency, scoreboards. Calmness IS the difference.
- **Inspiration refs (21st.dev):** Minimal-single-column layouts, content-default editorial blocks
