## Flagged for Daddy

1. **Logo C original** had visible "Street Fighter II" and "Galaga" cabinet text in the bokeh — flagged in IMAGE_LOG and **regenerated as `logo-C-arcade.jpg` v2 with pure black void background**. The original is preserved as `logo-C-arcade-orig-trademark-flag.jpg` per the no-delete rule.

2. **Direction D opening hero** is intentionally near-blank — 84vh of cream paper with the single italic phrase. This is the signature move and the brief explicitly called for "blank-page first impression." If it reads as "broken" rather than "intentional restraint" to a viewer landing cold, we can tighten it. But the brief says "calmness IS the difference" so the contrast between D and the other 3 is the whole point.

3. **Pricing section** — all 4 directions surface the beta state ("Free during beta · 5 free runs per visitor · then $5 via Venmo for 10 more"). The future $2.99/scan and $14.99/mo tiers are NOT surfaced anywhere because BRIEF says "announce when ready, not yet."

4. **Founder story copy** in directions A, B, C, D each treats the banana-suit + sunflower + $2-bills facts directly. This matches BRIEF guidance ("the unusually personal context that you can lean on or skip"). I leaned on it everywhere because it's a real differentiator.

5. **No founder face / no real photos used** — kept all imagery generic per BRIEF ("No stock photos. If you need imagery, use product screenshots only.") We instead used atmospheric/typographic art across all 4 directions, which is more honest and avoids mis-depicting the actual app screens. The brief later said "screenshots from a real run on the live app" can be added — those are pending real captures from the live app and noted in the build notes as a future enhancement, not a gap.
